Nutrition Blog

A balanced diet is one that fulfills all of a person's nutritional needs. Humans need a certain amount of calories and nutrients to stay healthy. A balanced diet provides all the nutrients a person requires, without going over the recommended daily calorie intake. an example of a balanced diet is a balanced meal including vegetables and fruits that must form about half of the human's plate serving since vegetables and fruits are the healthiest foods to eat. The rest of the food plate serving should contain proteins and grains.
